Our Community

A Treasured Waterfront Community

Eagle Harbor is a rare and treasured community along the Patuxent Riverâ€”one of a few historic waterfront African American communities remaining in the United States. Founded in 1929, our town has been a place where the rush of city life gives way to peace, natural splendor, and a sense of belonging.

From the sunrise over the Patuxent River to eagles soaring above, Eagle Harbor offers a daily escape and serves as a reminder of life's simple joys. We are committed to preserving our heritage while providing a welcoming space for residents and visitors.
