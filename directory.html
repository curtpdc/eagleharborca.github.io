How to Get Directory Access

1

Verify Residency

Confirm you are a current Eagle Harbor resident with property ownership or rental agreement documentation.

2

Contact Board

Reach out to the Citizens Association board through our contact form or attend a monthly town meeting.

3

Receive Credentials

Once verified, you'll receive your login credentials via email or at the next community meeting.

4

Access Directory

Log in to view contact information, connect with neighbors, and stay updated on community matters.

Directory Features

Contact Information

Access phone numbers, email addresses, and mailing addresses for all community members.

Search & Filter

Easily find specific residents by name, address, or contact information using our search tools.

Address Mapping

View resident locations on an interactive community map to better connect with neighbors.

Privacy Protected

All information is secure and only accessible to verified community members with proper credentials.

Mobile Friendly

Access the directory from any device - desktop, tablet, or smartphone for convenience.

Regular Updates

Directory information is regularly updated to ensure accuracy and current contact details.

Directory Usage Guidelines

Respectful Use

Use contact information respectfully and only for community-related purposes. Respect privacy and personal boundaries.

No Commercial Use

Directory information may not be used for commercial purposes, solicitation, or marketing activities.

Confidentiality

Do not share directory information with non-residents or use it outside of the Eagle Harbor community.

Report Issues

Report any misuse of directory information or privacy concerns to the Citizens Association board immediately.
